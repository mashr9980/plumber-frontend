Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our plumbing services.

For plumbing emergencies, we typically arrive within 1-2 hours of your call, depending on your location and current demand. We prioritize emergency calls and have plumbers available 24/7 to handle urgent situations such as burst pipes, major leaks, or sewer backups.

We provide free estimates for most standard plumbing jobs. For more complex projects, we may charge a diagnostic fee which is then applied to the cost of the job if you choose to proceed with our services. We always provide clear pricing information before beginning any work.

Yes, we stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. We offer a 90-day warranty on labor and up to 1-year warranty on parts, depending on the manufacturer's warranty. If any issues arise with our plumbing work, we'll come back and fix it at no additional cost.

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and electronic transfers. For larger projects, we also offer flexible payment plans to help make your plumbing services more affordable.

For water-related emergencies, locate and shut off the water supply to the affected area or at the main shut-off valve. For gas leaks, evacuate the premises immediately and call both us and your gas company from a safe location. For overflowing toilets, turn off the water supply valve behind the toilet. While waiting for our arrival, try to minimize water damage by using towels or buckets to collect water.
